Publication of the accepted papers in the conference proceedings is subject to:

  • Completion of registration formalities within due date
  • Submission of camera-ready manuscript strictly following the reviewers’ comments, and as per format of Springer book series “Lecture Notes on Data Engineering and Communications Technologies” (LaTeX/MS Word and PDF version) through conference email-ID
  • Submission of duly filled-in Registration form, payment proof and Author’s agreement form through conference email-ID
  • Uploading Camera ready manuscript PDF in Easychair
  • All the above within -02. 12. 2021.

According to IEM-ICDC 2021 conference registration policy, at least one author of each accepted paper must complete registration formalities including payment of registration fees at the specified rate given in the website.

Please complete the registration within 02. 12. 2021 and upload Manuscript PDF in Easychair. The following documents are to be emailed to conference email id iem-icdc@iemcal.com in a zip file:

  • 1.Scanned copy of the payment proof
  • 2.Filled-up and signed Author’s agreement form
  • 3.Filled-up and signed registration form
  • 4.Camera-ready paper both in word/LaTeX as well as PDF format

All presented papers will be submitted to Springer for their final acceptance and publication where decision of the Springer will be final and if Springer disagree to publish any paper presented and uploaded, there will be no refund of registration fees.

All chapter authors (corresponding authors, co-authors, contributing authors) should include their affiliation and email address in their manuscript. Their email addresses (also published in the chapters) will be used by Springer to provide authors with a personal MySpringer account where they can download a free copy of the eBook.
