The conference proceedings published in Scopus indexed Springer Book series "Lecture Notes in Data Engineering and Communication Technologies"

https://www.springer.com/series/15362

Indexing: The books of this series are submitted to SCOPUS, ISI Proceedings, MetaPress, Springerlink and DBLP.

Due to the pandemic situation of COVID-19, the conference was being organized as a virtual event, where participants can present their research papers, attend technical sessions and the keynote speeches, participate in workshop, all in online mode.

Date: 25th-27th September, 2020.
